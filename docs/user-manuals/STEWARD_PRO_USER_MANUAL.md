# Steward Pro Institutional Terminal - User Manual

## 1. Introduction
Welcome to **Steward Pro**, an institutional-grade trading environment designed for both high-precision execution and advanced user training. This manual covers the core operational modules of the platform, focusing on the **Tactical Hub** and the **Institutional Terminal**.

---

## 2. Global Navigation
The sidebar has been updated with professional-grade terminology to reflect the maturity of the system:
*   **Tactical Hub**: The primary manual execution and training center.
*   **Institutional Terminal**: Advanced, high-density terminal for professional market analysis.
*   **Algo Mandates**: The deployment zone for autonomous AI strategies.
*   **Wealth Vault**: Your unified portfolio and liquidity management center.

---

## 3. Tactical Hub (Steward Pro)
The Tactical Hub is designed for "Strategic Execution." It bridges the gap between raw manual trading and AI-assisted governance.

### 3.1 Training Academy Mode 🎓
**To Enable**: Click the "Enter Academy" button in the header.
*   **Sentinel Insights**: Enabling this mode activates a layer of AI-driven tooltips and "Training Tips" (in amber).
*   **Educational Context**: The platform will explain *why* certain technical signals are bullish or bearish (e.g., explaining RSI divergence or MACD crossovers).
*   **Target Audience**: Perfect for training users or demoing the platform to non-expert testers.

### 3.2 Security Handshake Protocol 🤝
Every trade executed in Steward Pro undergoes a **Security Handshake**.
*   **What it is**: A high-fidelity animation that simulates validating "Order Fingerprints" across distributed nodes.
*   **Purpose**: This provides a "Premium/Mature" feel to the execution process, instilling confidence that every trade is securely validated before being sent to the exchange.

### 3.3 Sentiment & Technical Pulse
*   **Sentiment Gauge**: A radial visualization showing the balance between Bullish and Bearish volume in real-time.
*   **Technical Pulse**: A live breakdown of indicators (RSI, SMA, Bollinger Bands) relative to the current timeframe.
*   **Sentinel Feed**: A terminal-style log window that shows the "thoughts" of the Steward AI as it processes market data.

## 4. Institutional Terminal
The Terminal is a high-density environment designed for traders who need deep market data.

*   **Order Book Depth**: A real-time visualization of Bids (green) and Asks (red). The background depth bars indicate where liquidity is strongest.
*   **Master Execution Panel**:
    *   **Side Selection**: Large, tactile Buy/Sell toggles.
    *   **Asset Classes**: Support for Equities, Derivatives, Commodities, and Forex.
    *   **Bracket Orders**: Use the range sliders to set **Stop Loss** and **Take Profit** targets simultaneously during order entry.
*   **Live Metrics Panel**: Displays sub-millisecond execution speeds, Signal Confidence percentages, and API status.

---

## 5. Wealth Vault (Portfolio Overview)
The Wealth Vault manages your institutional liquidity.
*   **Vault Value**: Real-time calculation of invested capital vs. available cash.
*   **Fund Management**: Unified "Deposit" and "Withdraw" flows with transaction confirmation.
*   **Asset Allocation**: A pie-chart breakdown showing your diversification across symbols and sectors.
*   **Active Positions**: Real-time P/L tracking for every open position.

---

## 6. Pro Tips for Testers
1.  **Switch to Autonomous**: Click the "Autonomous" button in the header to let the AI take over. The platform will then manage trades based on your **Algo Mandates**.
2.  **Use the Overlays**: In Training Mode, pay attention to the floating "Training Tips." They are designed to turn a tester into a pro trader in minutes.
3.  **Low Latency**: Watch the "Master Node" latency in the bottom ticker—it demonstrates the high-performance nature of the backend.

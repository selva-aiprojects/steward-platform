# StockSteward AI: Auditor / Compliance Manual
## Role: Compliance & Risk Oversight

Welcome to **Compliance Oversight**. Your interface is specialized for read-only verification, audit integrity, and regulatory reporting.

### 1. Audit Integrity
- **Global Audit Trail**: View every trade, decision, and system modification with precise timestamps.
- **Decision Logic**: Inspect the exact "Chain of Thought" the AI used to execute a specific trade.
- **Cryptographic Verification**: Confirm that logs have not been altered or tampered with.

### 2. Reporting
- **Risk Exposure Reports**: Generate reports on sectoral concentration and leverage status.
- **Regulatory Exports**: Standardized PDF exports for SEC/FINRA compliance reviews.

### 3. Restrictions
- **Read-Only Access**: As an Auditor, you cannot execute trades or modify system configurations. This ensures strict "Separation of Duties" requirements.

> [!NOTE]
> All audit logs are mirrored to a secure off-site repository for maximum reliability.
